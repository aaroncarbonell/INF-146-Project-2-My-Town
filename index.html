Welcome to the borough of Fort Lee. One of the largest and fastest growing ethnic Korean enclaves outside of Korea. This town serves as a modern relic of the American Revolution as well as location for America's busiest bridge, the George Washington Bridge (GWB) having two levels of traffic: one above and the other below.
